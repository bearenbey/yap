# YAP (Yet Another Planner)

A personal productivity app with task management, mind mapping, and markdown notes. All data stays on your device.

## Links

- [Privacy Policy](privacy)
- [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.yapapp.android)

## Features

- Kanban-style task management with 4 stages
- Interactive mind map with group hierarchy
- Markdown notes with tag organization
- Daily, weekly & monthly digest
- Full-text search across tasks & notes
- Share tasks & notes via Android share sheet
- Export all tasks as CSV
- Dark and light themes
